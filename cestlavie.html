C’est La Vie: The Game of Social Life

Your game id: Unique identified of your character profile. There are 64 different profiles in this game.
Total: At the end of the game you will record your total points here.
Game progress: Displays your progress through the 16 stages of the game. /21
Your character profile:Your profile describes your character’s gender and sexual orientation, race/ethnicity, citizenship status, special conditions and disabilities, money, bonuses, and voting status.
Vote: Determines whether or not your character is eligible to vote in elections.
Social groups: Your character is associated with one or more social groups. These groups represent various social categories related to race (V, W, and Q), gender (V, W, M, N), sexual orientation (V, W, P), and ability/disability (V, W, S). The exact nature of these codes will be explained at the end of the game. Membership in various groups may affect the choices available to you during game play.
Neighborhood: Will be determined during game play.
School: Will be determined during game play.
Money: Each money unit ($) is currency that can be used to make purchases. You can earn more money under some circumstances.
Debt: In some situations (but not all), you may be able to use credit or a loan to make a purchase – however, interest penalties will apply. As you gain money in the game, you will need to use this money to pay off your debt. Money cannot be gained until all debt is paid off. You cannot be more than 20$ in debt at any point in time.
Bonuses: Bonuses represent favours you can ask to other people. They're accounted in a similar way to money.
Experience: Represents your education.
Wellness: Represents your positive health.
Illness: Anytime that you experience negative wellness credits (you have lost more wellness credits than you have acquired), these negative wellness credits will be reflected on your score card as illness. One must overcome illness before benefitting from wellness.
